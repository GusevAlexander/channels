Stage 1 consists of a task in which you will be translating a sequence of numbers into a series of letters from the English alphabet.
On each screen, you will see
  1. a sequence of NUMBERS
  2. a KEY indicating which LETTER represents each NUMBER
  3. a text box in which the letter solution should be typed.
On each screen, a new sequence of numbers will appear along with a new key of number to letter relationships. Note that the keys will likely be different on each screen.
First you will participate in a practice round to become familiar with the task. All participants in your group will engage in this practice round, including all 3 “{% bps %}” participants and the 1 “{% op %}” participant. This is so you are all familiar with the ways in which an individual can succeed at the task, and so you all can gauge how challenging the task is.
After the practice round, all participants will be informed of how their bonus is determined based on performance in the (“Live”) round of the task.
