  1. The goal is to enter the sequence of letters that correctly corresponds to the sequence of numbers provided, using the KEY on the top of the screen.
  2. All number sequences provided are 8 numbers long
  3. All correct letters sequences are 8 letters long
  4. The KEY describing the mapping of numbers to letters will change on each screen (each decoding puzzle)
  5. After you have entered the sequence of letters you must click the submit button.
  6. It is possible to enter an incorrect sequence of letters (i.e., if you click submit with an incorrect sequence of letters entered, the game will NOT notify you of your incorrect sequence prior to submission)
  7. Only CORRECTLY solved Decoding Puzzles will increase your total number of solved puzzles
  8. There will be a counter on the bottom of the screen indicating the total number of Decoding Puzzles solved.
  9. The Live Round will run for {{ Constants.task_time_seconds|minutes }}. You will have a countdown clock on each screen so you can track your remaining time.
  10. At no point will you or the {% op %} know the performance (number of successful decoded puzzles) of any of the other players.
  11. When all {% bps %} have clicked “Ready” the Live Round will begin.
